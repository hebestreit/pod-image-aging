Thank you for installing pod-image-aging.

In case you have any questions, please refer to the documentation:
https://github.com/hebestreit/pod-image-aging

Once installed you can list all pods with their image age by running:

  $  kubectl get pods -A -o json | jq '[. | {pod: .items[].metadata}][] | {name: .pod.name, images: .pod.annotations["pod-image-aging.hbst.io/status"] | fromjson}'
